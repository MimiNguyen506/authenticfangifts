⚠️ Real vs. Fake: How to Spot Unlicensed Star Wars Merch

Before you buy anywhere — including marketplaces — here's what to check:

  • Price under $12: Almost certainly unlicensed. Licensed Star Wars manufacturing has real costs.
  • No official hang tag or label: Licensed Star Wars products have printed licensing labels on the inside collar.
  • Off-proportion logos: The Star Wars wordmark has exact kerning and stroke weights. Bootlegs get these slightly wrong.
  • New seller, no reviews: Counterfeit accounts appear and disappear constantly. No track record = high risk.
  • Can't find on official retailer list: Lucasfilm maintains a list of licensed apparel partners. SenPrints is on it.
