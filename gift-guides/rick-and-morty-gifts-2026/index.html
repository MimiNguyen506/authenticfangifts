⚠️ Real vs Fake Rick and Morty Merch — What to Watch For

The Rick and Morty counterfeit problem is real. Here's what to look for:

  • Price under $15 for a T-shirt — nearly always unlicensed
  • No brand/licensing info on the product page — red flag
  • Marketplace seller with low reviews or new account
  • Blurry thumbnail photos — hiding print quality issues
  • No Adult Swim or WBD trademark language in the product copy
