⚠️ Real vs. Fake Wonder Woman Merch — How to Spot the Difference

The Wonder Woman merch market is flooded with counterfeits. Here's what separates official licensed products from fakes:

  • Price: Licensed Wonder Woman T-shirts cost $28–$35 USD. Under $15 on a marketplace = almost certainly counterfeit.
  • Quality: Official screen prints won't crack, peel, or ghost after washing. Bootleg prints fail within weeks.
  • Licensing language: Look for "Officially Licensed by DC Comics / Warner Bros." — not just "inspired by" or "fan art."
  • Seller history: Licensed retailers have verifiable track records. One-week-old Etsy shops with 200 five-star reviews are often fraudulent.
  • Design accuracy: Official DC designs meet strict brand guidelines — colors, proportions, and character depictions are pixel-perfect. Fakes are slightly off in ways fans notice immediately.
